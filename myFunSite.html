What is the Fried Company?

We are a company that specializes in the critique of different fried chicken products from all over the United States. We are hoping to expand to products from all over the world such as Korean fried chicken, Taiwanese popcorn chicken, etc.

How does it work?

Everyone that works for our company has underwent some form of training to specialize in critiquing crispiness, texture, flavor, spice, and many other elements. Upon your request, our professionals arrive at your restaurant or wherever and test your chicken. We send a finalized report with all the scores two days after the test. The better the quality of your chicken, the better rating we give you.
